What is Arpentage?

A collective reading method born in the French labor movement. The book is physically divided among participants. Each person reads their section and shares reactions. Collective intelligence emerges.

The 4 Steps

  • 1. ‚úÇÔ∏è Divide the book into sections (15-20 pages each)
  • 2. üìñ Individual reading (30-45 minutes)
  • 3. üí¨ Sharing circle ‚Äî reactions, not summaries
  • 4. üß† Collective synthesis ‚Äî intelligence emerges

Why It Works

Nobody reads the whole book. Everyone brings their perspective. The lived experience is valued as much as expertise. Knowledge is democratized.

What is ECP?

"√âconomie Contributive Provisionn√©e" ‚Äî a model allowing people on unemployment, disability, or welfare to contribute to social projects without losing their benefits.

How It Works

  • Contributors participate in social projects (education, mutual aid, culture)
  • Their contribution is valued and "provisioned" (recorded but not paid immediately)
  • They keep their social benefits (INAMI, ONEM, CPAS)
  • When stable, the provision can be converted to income

The Numbers

  • 292,790 unemployed receiving benefits
  • 158,697 on welfare (RIS)
  • ~978,000 people who could contribute
